5. Results service enhancements

  • On save: placement <= competition.maxPlacements
  • Auto-fill points_awarded from settings: 1→points_first, 2→points_second, 3→points_third, 4→points_fourth
  • On publish: reject duplicate (competition_id, placement) among published rows
  • GROUP results: prefer team_display_name on leader registration when participant_profile_id is null
  • Optional: POST /api/event-competition-results/publish-by-competition/{competitionId} bulk publish

7. Security & tenancy

  • Team captain: may POST /team only when registered_by_user_profile_id matches JWT user profile and competition is GROUP.
  • Guardian: group member participants must have guardian_user_profile_id matching caller (YOUTH).
  • Group member CRUD: admin or registration owner until status is CONFIRMED.
  • Public read: competitions with new fields; published results unchanged.
