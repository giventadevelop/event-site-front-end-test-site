# AI Image Editor Prompt: Hero Section Combined Image (Section 1)

Use this prompt in AI image tools (e.g. Nano banana, DALL·E, Midjourney, etc.) to generate or edit the combined hero image for the left panel (Section 1) of the hero layout.

---

## Copy-paste prompt (full)

```
Create a single combined image for a website hero left panel. Composition:

SCENE (base): A serene Kerala backwaters scene. Center the view on a traditional wooden boat and a tall coconut tree; show a strip of calm water (river/lake) so boat and tree are the main focus. Crop or soften the rest (sky, distant shore) so the boat and coconut tree dominate. Warm golden-green tones, soft daylight, peaceful mood.

LOGO PLACEMENT: The organization logo (Malayalees US / Malayalee.US style) is placed in the center of the image, over the scene, clearly visible and readable, as if it belongs in the scene. Logo should feel integrated, not floating.

BRANDING TEXT: The words "MALAYALEE.US" appear vertically along the left side of the image, beside or near the coconut tree, as elegant vertical typography (one letter per line or stacked). Clean, readable font; color that contrasts with the greenery (e.g. white, cream, or warm gold). It should read as subtle branding, not overpowering the scene.

CULTURAL BLEND: The image should feel like a blend of Kerala (India) and American identity—a US-based Malayalee organization. Keep the scene clearly Kerala (boat, coconut tree, backwaters). Add a very subtle American touch only: e.g. a faint hint of US flag colors in the light or reflection, or a very subtle star/motif in the corner, or soft red-white-blue accent in the logo/text treatment. The American element must be minor; Kerala remains the dominant visual.

FORMAT: Portrait orientation, tall/narrow aspect ratio (e.g. 3:4 or 2:3), suitable for a narrow left column in a split hero layout. High quality, suitable for web hero use.
```
