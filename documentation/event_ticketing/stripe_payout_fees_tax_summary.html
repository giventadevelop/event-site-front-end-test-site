Stripe Payout, Fees, and Tax Summary

Document Date: January 11, 2026

Purpose: Clarify Stripe payout amounts, timing, fees, and tax responsibilities for ticket purchases.

Example: $1.00 Ticket Purchase

This document uses a $1.00 ticket purchase as an example to illustrate Stripe's fee structure and payout calculations.

1. Stripe Fee Structure

1.1 Standard Online Payment Fee

For online card payments in the United States, Stripe charges:

  • 2.9% of the transaction amount
  • $0.30 fixed fee per transaction

Example Calculation: $1.00 Ticket

Item Amount Calculation
Ticket Price (Customer Pays) $1.00 Base amount
Stripe Processing Fee (2.9%) $0.029 $1.00 × 0.029 = $0.029
Stripe Fixed Fee $0.30 Fixed per transaction
Total Stripe Fee $0.329 $0.029 + $0.30
Net Payout to Your Bank $0.671 $1.00 - $0.329 = $0.671

⚠️ Important Note for Small Transactions

For very small transactions like $1.00, the fixed $0.30 fee represents 30% of the transaction amount. This is why Stripe's fee structure can be expensive for low-value transactions.

Recommendation: Consider setting a minimum ticket price (e.g., $5.00 or $10.00) to ensure reasonable profit margins after Stripe fees.

1.2 Additional Fees

Stripe does NOT charge any additional fees beyond the processing fee. However, there are some optional services that may incur costs:

  • Instant Payouts: 1% fee (optional, for same-day payouts)
  • Disputes/Chargebacks: $15.00 per dispute (only if customer disputes the charge)
  • International Cards: Additional 1% fee for cards issued outside your country
  • Currency Conversion: 2% fee if accepting payments in different currencies

2. Tax Collection and Responsibility

2.1 Who Pays Tax?

Stripe does NOT pay tax on your behalf. Tax collection and remittance are the merchant's responsibility (your responsibility).

2.2 How Tax Works with Stripe

  • Stripe Tax (Optional Service): Stripe offers an optional "Stripe Tax" service that automatically calculates and collects sales tax from customers based on their location. This is a separate service that you must enable.
  • Tax Amount: If Stripe Tax is enabled, the tax amount is collected from the customer and included in the transaction total.
  • Tax Remittance: You (the merchant) are responsible for remitting the collected tax to the appropriate tax authorities. Stripe Tax helps calculate and collect it, but you must file and pay the tax.
  • Tax on Fees: Stripe does NOT charge tax on their processing fees. The fee is simply deducted from your payout.

Example: $1.00 Ticket with 8% Sales Tax

Item Amount Who Pays
Ticket Price $1.00 Customer
Sales Tax (8%) $0.08 Customer (collected by you)
Total Customer Pays $1.08 Customer
Stripe Fee (on $1.08) $0.331 You (deducted from payout)
Net Payout to Your Bank $0.749 You receive
Tax Collected $0.08 You must remit to tax authority
Your Net Revenue (after tax) $0.669 $0.749 - $0.08 (tax to remit)

3. Payout Timing

3.1 Standard Payout Schedule

Stripe's standard payout schedule depends on your account type and location:

Account Type Payout Timing Details
Standard Account (US) 2-7 business days First payout may take up to 7-14 days for new accounts
Express Account 1-2 business days Faster payouts for verified accounts
Instant Payouts Same day (minutes to hours) Optional service with 1% fee

3.2 Payout Settlement Process

  1. Transaction Occurs: Customer purchases ticket ($1.00)
  2. Authorization: Stripe immediately authorizes the payment (funds are "held")
  3. Settlement: Stripe settles the transaction (typically within 1-2 business days)
  4. Payout Initiated: Stripe initiates payout to your bank account
  5. Bank Processing: Your bank processes the ACH transfer (1-3 business days)
  6. Funds Available: Funds appear in your bank account

Typical Timeline for $1.00 Ticket Purchase:

  • Day 0: Customer purchases ticket, payment authorized
  • Day 1-2: Stripe settles the transaction
  • Day 2-4: Stripe initiates payout (standard account)
  • Day 4-7: Bank processes ACH transfer
  • Day 5-9: Funds available in your bank account

Total Time: Approximately 5-9 business days from purchase to funds in your bank account.

3.3 Factors Affecting Payout Timing

  • Account Age: New accounts may have longer hold periods (up to 14 days)
  • Risk Level: High-risk transactions may be held longer
  • Weekends/Holidays: Business days only (weekends and holidays don't count)
  • Bank Processing: Your bank's ACH processing time (typically 1-3 business days)

4. Stripe Balance Transaction Fields

When you retrieve a BalanceTransaction from Stripe API, you'll see these key fields:

Field Description Example ($1.00 Ticket)
amount Gross amount of the transaction (in cents) 100 cents ($1.00)
fee Stripe's processing fee (in cents) 33 cents ($0.33)
net Net payout amount = amount - fee (in cents) 67 cents ($0.67)
available_on Date when funds will be available in your Stripe balance 2026-01-15 (example)
status Transaction status (pending, available, paid_out) pending → available → paid_out

Key Formula:

Net Payout = Transaction Amount - Stripe Fee

net = amount - fee

For $1.00 ticket: $0.67 = $1.00 - $0.33

6. Recommendations

6.1 For Low-Value Transactions

  • Set Minimum Ticket Price: Consider $5.00 or $10.00 minimum to ensure reasonable margins
  • Bundle Tickets: Offer multi-ticket discounts to increase transaction value
  • Consider Alternative Payment Methods: ACH transfers have lower fees for larger amounts

6.2 For Tax Collection

  • Enable Stripe Tax: Automatically calculate and collect sales tax
  • Set Up Tax Remittance: Register with tax authorities and file returns regularly
  • Track Tax Collected: Maintain records of all tax collected for reporting

6.3 For Faster Payouts

  • Upgrade to Express Account: Faster payouts (1-2 business days)
  • Use Instant Payouts: Same-day payouts for 1% fee (good for cash flow)
  • Maintain Good Account Standing: Avoid disputes and high-risk transactions

7. Database Fields Mapping

In your event_ticket_transaction table, these fields correspond to Stripe data:

Database Field Stripe Source Description Example ($1.00 Ticket)
final_amount PaymentIntent.amount Total amount customer paid $1.00
stripe_fee_amount BalanceTransaction.fee Stripe processing fee $0.33
stripe_amount_tax PaymentIntent.amount_details.tax or CheckoutSession.total_details.amount_tax Tax collected from customer (if Stripe Tax enabled) $0.08 (if 8% tax)
net_payout_amount BalanceTransaction.net Net amount you receive (final_amount - stripe_fee_amount) $0.67

⚠️ Important Calculation Note:

Net Payout Amount = Final Amount - Stripe Fee Amount

Tax is NOT deducted from the payout. Tax is collected from the customer and must be remitted separately by you.

Example: If customer pays $1.08 ($1.00 + $0.08 tax), you receive $0.75 ($1.08 - $0.33 fee), and you must remit $0.08 tax to the tax authority.
