Stripe Multi-Ticket Purchase Breakdown

Document Date: January 11, 2026

Example: Purchasing 2 tickets ($1.00 + $2.00 = $3.00 total) in a single transaction

Purchase Scenario

  • Ticket 1: $1.00
  • Ticket 2: $2.00
  • Total Transaction Amount: $3.00
  • Payment Method: Single Stripe transaction (one checkout)

1. Single Transaction Calculation

Key Point: Stripe Charges ONE Fee Per Transaction

When you purchase multiple tickets in a single checkout, Stripe treats it as one transaction and charges the processing fee once on the total amount.

Breakdown: $3.00 Total Purchase

Item Amount Calculation
Ticket 1 $1.00 Individual ticket price
Ticket 2 $2.00 Individual ticket price
Subtotal (Before Stripe Fee) $3.00 $1.00 + $2.00
Stripe Processing Fee (2.9%) $0.087 $3.00 × 0.029 = $0.087
Stripe Fixed Fee $0.30 Fixed per transaction (charged once)
Total Stripe Fee $0.387 $0.087 + $0.30
Net Payout to Your Bank $2.613 $3.00 - $0.387 = $2.613

2. Comparison: Single Transaction vs. Separate Transactions

Scenario A: Single Transaction (Recommended)

Item Amount
Total Purchase $3.00
Stripe Fee (2.9% + $0.30) $0.387
Net Payout $2.613
Fee Percentage 12.9% of transaction

Scenario B: Two Separate Transactions (Not Recommended)

Transaction Amount Stripe Fee Net Payout
Transaction 1 ($1.00 ticket) $1.00 $0.329 $0.671
Transaction 2 ($2.00 ticket) $2.00 $0.358 $1.642
Total $3.00 $0.687 $2.313
Fee Percentage 22.9% of total (much higher!)

⚠️ Key Insight:

Single Transaction is MUCH Better!

  • Single transaction: $2.613 net payout (fee: $0.387)
  • Two transactions: $2.313 net payout (fee: $0.687)
  • Savings: $0.30 more in your pocket with single transaction!

Why? The $0.30 fixed fee is charged once in a single transaction, but twice if you split into separate transactions.

3. With Sales Tax (8% Example)

Breakdown: $3.00 Purchase + 8% Sales Tax

Item Amount Who Pays
Ticket 1 $1.00 Customer
Ticket 2 $2.00 Customer
Subtotal $3.00 Customer
Sales Tax (8%) $0.24 Customer (collected by you)
Total Customer Pays $3.24 Customer
Stripe Fee (2.9% of $3.24) $0.094 You (deducted from payout)
Stripe Fixed Fee $0.30 You (deducted from payout)
Total Stripe Fee $0.394 You
Net Payout to Your Bank $2.846 You receive
Tax Collected $0.24 You must remit to tax authority
Your Net Revenue (after tax remittance) $2.606 $2.846 - $0.24 (tax to remit)

Important Tax Note:

Stripe calculates the fee on the total amount including tax ($3.24), not just the ticket prices ($3.00). This is because the customer pays $3.24 total, and Stripe's fee is based on what the customer actually pays.

4. Database Fields for Multi-Ticket Purchase

In your event_ticket_transaction table, a single transaction record represents the entire purchase:

Database Field Value Description
quantity 2 Number of tickets purchased
total_amount $3.00 Sum of individual ticket prices ($1.00 + $2.00)
tax_amount $0.24 Sales tax collected (if applicable)
final_amount $3.24 Total amount customer paid (including tax)
stripe_fee_amount $0.394 Stripe processing fee (on $3.24 total)
stripe_amount_tax $0.24 Tax collected (if Stripe Tax enabled)
net_payout_amount $2.846 Net amount you receive ($3.24 - $0.394)

5. Stripe Balance Transaction Details

When you retrieve the BalanceTransaction from Stripe API for this purchase:

Stripe Field Value (in cents) Value (in dollars) Description
amount 324 cents $3.24 Gross transaction amount (what customer paid)
fee 39 cents $0.39 Stripe processing fee (rounded from $0.394)
net 285 cents $2.85 Net payout amount (rounded from $2.846)

Note on Rounding:

Stripe stores amounts in cents (integers), so there may be slight rounding differences. The net field is the most accurate representation of what you'll receive.

6. Payout Timeline

Timeline for $3.00 Multi-Ticket Purchase

  • Day 0: Customer purchases 2 tickets ($3.00 total), payment authorized
  • Day 1-2: Stripe settles the transaction
  • Day 2-4: Stripe initiates payout (standard account)
  • Day 4-7: Bank processes ACH transfer
  • Day 5-9: $2.85 (approximately) available in your bank account

Total Time: Approximately 5-9 business days from purchase to funds in your bank account.

8. Recommendations

8.1 For Multi-Ticket Purchases

  • Always use single checkout: Encourage customers to purchase all tickets in one transaction
  • Offer bundle discounts: Incentivize multi-ticket purchases to increase transaction value
  • Set minimum quantities: Consider requiring minimum 2 tickets per purchase

8.2 For Fee Optimization

  • Higher transaction values: The $0.30 fixed fee becomes a smaller percentage on larger purchases
  • Example: $10 purchase = $0.59 fee (5.9%), $100 purchase = $3.20 fee (3.2%)
  • Encourage larger purchases: Offer discounts for buying 5+ tickets
