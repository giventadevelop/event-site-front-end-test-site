Executive summary

Problem and solution

Organizations run events with one or many competitions (dance, music, sports trials, talent shows, adult contests). Registrants may be parents registering children, adults registering themselves, or team captains for group events. After judging, organizers publish winners with placement, prize details, and photos.

Today the platform supports general ticket checkout only—not competition catalogs, participant profiles, or structured results publishing.

Solution: Optional is_competition_event on event_details, event_competition_* tables, REST APIs, routes under /events/[id]/competitions/, and admin tools—without overloading event_ticket_type.

Open questions (stakeholder sign-off)

  1. Per-competition fee only vs single event pass?
  2. Stripe required at launch vs admin confirm / $0 only?
  3. Fixed four grade categories (YOUTH) vs tenant-configurable divisions?
  4. Group billing: one leader pays vs split fees?
  5. Public results: full name vs initials vs codes? (stricter default for YOUTH)
  6. Max placements per competition: 3 only vs extended podium?
  7. Winner photos: required before publish or optional?
  8. Allow ticket sales when is_competition_event=true (allow_ticket_sales)?
  9. MIXED events: same Stripe cart for tickets + competitions or separate?
